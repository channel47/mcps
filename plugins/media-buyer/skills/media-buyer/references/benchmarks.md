# Industry Benchmarks

Use as context, not hard policy. Prefer account history when available. Applies to both platforms.

## Quality Score CPC Pressure

| Quality Score | CPC Pressure vs QS 10 | Interpretation |
|---:|---:|---|
| 1-3 | 2.5x to 4.0x | Severe efficiency drag |
| 4-5 | 1.5x to 2.4x | Material optimization opportunity |
| 6-7 | 1.1x to 1.4x | Usually acceptable |
| 8-10 | 1.0x baseline | Strong auction efficiency |

## CTR Reference Bands (Search)

| Match Type | Typical CTR Range |
|---|---|
| Exact | 4% to 9% |
| Phrase | 3% to 7% |
| Broad | 2% to 6% |

## CPA Interpretation

| Performance vs Target CPA | Status |
|---|---|
| <= 90% of target | Efficient |
| 91%-120% of target | Watch |
| > 120% of target | Investigate / action |

## Ad Testing Lift

For single-ad ad groups, use a conservative modeled upside of 5%-15% CTR lift. Default to 10%.

## Notes

- Vertical variation is large — always compare against account baseline first.
- Benchmarks are directional and should be called out as estimates.
